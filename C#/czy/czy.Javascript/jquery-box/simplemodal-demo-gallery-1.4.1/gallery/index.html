Flickr Badge Viewer

A modal dialog configured to display images. Demonstrates the use of the onOpen and onClose callbacks as well as a handful of options, and custom styling.

The viewer also includes keyboard navigation; Previous Image: p or ←, Next Image: n or →, Close Viewer: ESC.

To use: edit this file and modify the Flickr badge script href to point to your own badge, or just include gallery.js and gallery.css on your page that already contains your Flickr badge.
